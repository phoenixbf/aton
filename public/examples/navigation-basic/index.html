In this sample we showcase a few navigation modes (orbit, first-person, device-orientation and VR).

A few of these may not be available, depending on device and type of connection. Try this sample on different devices and use buttons in the top toolbar.

For first-person, device-orientation and immersive VR a teleport indicator is shown: a double-tap (or double-click) or main trigger on VR controllers allow you to move into that location.
